Etymology.

The word car comes from the Latin word "Carrus" which means a wheeled vehicle
or the old English word "carre" which means a two-wheel cart.

Throughout history, people used various means of transport for example they used
animals like horses or even boats in the sea. They also used wheeled carts which were
driven by horses and other animals like donkeys.

It is in the 19th Century that the first modern car was made with an engine.
It was first made by Karl Benz who was a German engineer.
